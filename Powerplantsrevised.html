US Electric Power Generation

Biomass, Geothermal, and Solar Comparison

Map authored by Amelia Carmello

This map displays power generation capacity in MW (mega watts) from renewable energy sources across the United States. Click on symbols to view plant details.

Design Principle Used: Using visual variables such as symbol size to communicate data clearly.
Change Made: I added a legend explaining the proportional circle sizes that represent power plant capacity.
Improved User Experience: The legend helps users understand how circle size relates to megawatt capacity, making it easier to compare power plants across the map.
